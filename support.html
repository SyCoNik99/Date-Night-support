Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your Date Night Pro subscription will be restored automatically if you have an active subscription through the App Store or Google Play.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your App Store or Google Play account. Go to your device's Settings → Subscriptions → Date Night to manage or cancel.

How do I share a date with my partner?

When viewing a date idea, tap the share button to send the details directly to your partner via text, email, or any messaging app.

Is my data private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your device. If the issue persists, please contact us and describe what's happening so we can help.
